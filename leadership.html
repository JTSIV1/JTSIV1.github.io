Jila - Backend Tech Lead

September 2024 - December 2024 Leadership Champaign, IL

Jila is an app aimed at helping local Q'anjob'al speakers gain better access to resources through more information in their own language. Q'anjob'al is an endangered native Mayan language spoken by a subset of the local immigrant community. As the Backend Tech Lead for Jila, I managed a development team of three using Vercel, REST API, and clerk to communicate with the ReactJS app and manage authentication. I also coordinated with project managers and other teams to ensure the success of the project.
